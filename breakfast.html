BACON APPLE PANCAKES

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 EGG
  • 1/2 CUP MILK
  • 2 TBSP BUTTER, MELTED
  • 6 SLICES APPLEWOOD SMOKED BACON, CHOPPED
  • 1 CUP PANCAKE MIX
  • 1 CUP SHREDDED APPLES
  • 1/4 TSP GROUND CINNAMON

DIRECTIONS

  1. BEAT TOGETHER EGG, MILK, AND MELTED BUTTER.
  2. STIR IN REMAINING INGREDIENTS; MIX WELL. ADD ADDITIONAL 2 TO 4 TABLESPOONS MILK FOR THINNER PANCAKES.
  3. POUR 1/4 CUP BATTER ON HOT GRIDDLE FOR EACH PANCAKE. TURN; COOK UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN.
